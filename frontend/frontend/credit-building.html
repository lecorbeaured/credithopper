Pro Tips for Success

Start with 1-2 Accounts

Don't open too many at once. Pick one credit builder (Kikoff or Self) and optionally one secured card. More isn't better - consistency is.

Set Up Auto-Pay

Payment history is 35% of your score. One late payment can undo months of progress. Set up automatic payments so you never miss.

Keep Utilization Low

If using a secured card, keep your balance under 30% of your limit. Under 10% is even better. Pay down before statement closes.

Be Patient

Most accounts need 3-6 months of history before significantly impacting your score. Stay consistent and the results will come.
