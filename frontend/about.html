Our Mission

The law is on your side. We help you use it.

Here's what the credit industry doesn't want you to know: when you dispute something on your credit report, the burden of proof is on THEM, not you. They have to verify it with documented evidence - or delete it. That's the law.

CreditHopper was built to put this power in the hands of everyday people. We give you the tools to hold credit bureaus and collectors accountable. No expensive lawyers, no $3,000 credit repair services - just you, armed with your legal rights and the letters to exercise them.

Our Story

Built by people who've been there

CreditHopper was founded by people who experienced the frustration of credit problems firsthand. We paid thousands to credit repair companies, watched them send generic letters, and saw mixed results. Then we learned the truth: the law was on our side the whole time.

We dug into the Fair Credit Reporting Act. We learned that bureaus can't just say "verified" without proof. We discovered that collectors have to validate debts or stop collecting. And we realized that a well-written letter demanding accountability gets results - because the burden is on them.

Today, we've helped over 50,000 users fight back against inaccurate credit reporting. But we're just getting started. Everyone deserves the chance to rebuild their credit and their life. That's what CreditHopper is about.
